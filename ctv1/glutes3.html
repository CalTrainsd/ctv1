Stiff-Legged Barbell Deadlift

Hold the bar with hands about 15” apart, your back straight, trunk flexed and head up. Use your lower back muscles to bend over until the bar nears your ankles, then return to the standing position.

Leg Press

Sit with the knees flexed about 90 degrees and your feet about shoulder-width apart on the platform, toes pointed slightly outward. Extend the legs, then return slowly to starting position.

Swiss Ball Hamstrings Curls

Lie on the floor supporting your upper body on your shoulders and arms held out to the sides. Place your heels on the top of the ball with your legs completely straight. Slowly bend your legs and then roll back to the starting position by extending your legs.
