Bicycle

Lie on your back with your hands behind your head and your knees toward your chest in a 45 degree angle. Make a pedaling motion with your legs, touching your right elbow to your left knee, then your left elbow to your left knee in a continuous motion.

cancel

Crunches

Lie on your back with your legs in the air and your knees bent, your hands behind your head and elbows out to the sides. Curl your upper body toward your knees by contracting your abs, pulling them tightly inward. Return with control to starting position.

Double Leg Stretch

Exhale, Lie on your back with your shins in table top position, parallel to the floor.Deepen the abs, bringing your forehead toward your knees. Grasp your shins. Inhale, reaching arms back and overhead as you extend legs at a 45 degree angle to the floor.
